We have stayed here three times and the overall condition of the place seems to have deteriorated to the point where it does not offer the minimal conditions for a comfortable stay. We stayed there this last month during a frigid cold weekend, our small room was very cold since the heater sputtered hot water and rattled but did not work. The bed was supposed to be full size for two adults but the blankets were too small and did not fit the bed. The toilet stopped working in the middle of the night and could not be fixed. Additionally, the shared bathrooms lack a sink or any kind of hand washing facilities, which means everybody has to return to their rooms to wash up in the small sink inside the room: I would guess every door knob in this hotel should be disinfected several times a day, but it isn't...
