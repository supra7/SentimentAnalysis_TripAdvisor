We visited New York and we stayed in the Marrakech Hotel. It was horrible. Dusty, cramped and the service was unfriendly. Our room was a joke - just the bed nothing else. It was nearly impossible to walk around the bed!!! After one night we demanded another room and after hours and some discussions with the manager got one which was a little bit bigger but also like a room in a hostel. After one more night we left the hotel and went to another....
