Stayed here, mainly because it was inexpensive and close to Kings Cross. Room was clean, and location is really convenient, but the noise from outside was non-stop and sleep depriving! Fair enough that the noise, from cars and streets, is outside the hotels control, but the noise insulation from walls and windows was pretty rubbish and made the problem worse. In additon, contrary to other stays in Travelodges, the double mattress was old with us both falling towards an unsupported centre of bed
