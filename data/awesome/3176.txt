Our plane landed late at night and we stayed for one night at this hotel. The shuttle was easy to find at the airport and came by every 15 minutes. The hotel staff was very friendly and they give you each a wonderful cookie when you check in. The room was beautiful and plenty big for 4 of us. The best part was the balcony that we realized we had in the morning. The view was beautiful. We were able to take the shuttle back to the airport the next morning to get our rental car. The hotel was clean, beds comfortable, and I thought the rates were very reasonable. We paid $145 with all fees. I definitely would stay here again.
