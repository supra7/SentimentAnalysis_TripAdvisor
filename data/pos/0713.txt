I loved the location of this hotel! Just a few steps away from the Market and every kind of food you could possibly want. I loved everything about the hotel: the bell staff was pleasant and helpful, check-in was a breeze, the room was absolutely gorgeous and well-appointed, a fine walking distance to everywhere we wanted to go. And the wonderful view from the rooftop lounge couldn't be beat. I only stayed for one night before a cruise, but I wished I could have stayed much longer. I had a partial water view on the front corner of the hotel (room 611, try this one out). I can't say enough about it, and I can't wait to stay here again. If you are on the fence about your next Seattle stay, book here. You won't be disappointed.
