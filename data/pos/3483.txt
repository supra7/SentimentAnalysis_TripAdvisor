This was my first trip to Seattle and I was traveling alone after a conference in Portland, Or. I found the hotel's location perfect for my purposes...very close to shopping areas and main attractions yet in a quiet, neighborhood section. The hotel's decor was understated elegance, comfortable, and very clean. The staff was excellent, always helpful and polite. One staff member, Mary, who was at the front desk and concierge desk went out of her way to be thoughtful and considerate. How nice to have someone remember your name and ask how your day's adventure went when you returned in the evening.
