We are a family of four and we stayed here twice. Location is great. Parking is 27.00 per day. Free coffee/tea in room. Nice pool and hot tub to do during your stay. Plus a gym. The room we got had two full beds. Smaller then queens but good for two kids and two adults. Clean room and good price for Seattle.
