An adequate hotel on the tram line from the ferries & train station up towards the Hippodrome etc. Also convenient for Bazzar. Comfortable clean rooms with satellite TV. Very small lounge area in reception. Roof terrace where breakfast is servered from which you can see the Golden Horn. Breakfast = "continental" : boiled eggs, bread, cold meats & cheese. Many good resturants in area - especially up hill behind hotel. Internet access in hotel and in road down hill.
