Hotel East Houston is yet another great choice in New York City. It is located in Lower Manhattan (Lower East Side), in close proximity to Broadway street = shopping district. The subway station is right around the corner and a huge grocery store about a block away. It takes less than 10 minutes to travel both ways, to financial district and to Upper Manhattan. Each of its room offers wi-fi and fiji water free of charg, breakfast buffet is served in dinigh area downstairs and hotel features an amazing rooftop with a view of Manhattan and its skyline. Despite the room size, guests feels very comfortable due to the modern design and easy accesibility to all the facilities.
