This hotel was well placed for the space needle and the monorail which were a few minutes walk away The monorail was a cheap and quick way to get downtown.. There was free tea and coffee available 24/7 in the lobby of the hotel also free internet.. The rooms were clean and the towels changed daily.The breakfast was complimentary and there was a good choice of food. Although there was no airport transportation the hotel were able to book transportation for you.
