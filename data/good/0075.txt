The Monaco was recommended by friends and was an excellent choice for hanging out in central Seattle without a car. Close to the waterfront, Pike St. Market and pretty much everything else we wanted to do for a first time to Seattle. The hotel was clean, comfortable and the staff wonderful; we never left the hotel without the doormen asking if we needed help with something. Applying for the Kimpton In Touch program got us a NYT delivered every morning and with free coffee in the lobby, well you've got me as a regular! Although, they pretty much had me at the leopard print robe...
