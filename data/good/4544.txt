I would not hesitate to return to this hotel. We found the staff charming, helpful and sincere. It is an older hotel, but I prefer quiet, peaceful and friendly. We found most hotels nearby overcrowded, loud and exposed to passerbys. This hotel has large private courtyard with sun or shade. There is a swimming pool, a snack bar and outdoor Tiki Bar. Hawaian music and dance is performed nightly. The night air is filled with the fragrance of plumeria and tuberose. Ahhh...
